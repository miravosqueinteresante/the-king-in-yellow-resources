## Call of Cthulhu

The King in Yellow is a major element in Chaosium's Call of Cthulhu RPG. Key scenarios include:

### The King in Yellow (1989)

A full campaign by Chaosium that adapts the four play-linked stories into investigative scenarios. Players encounter the Yellow Sign, explore Carcosa, and confront the King himself.

### No Man's Land (1996)

A scenario set in WWI France where the King in Yellow mythos intersects with the horrors of trench warfare.

## Delta Green

The modern-day conspiracy RPG Delta Green has incorporated the King in Yellow through the "Hastur" mythos. Delta Green's version treats the Yellow Sign as a memetic hazard — seeing it is itself a form of contamination.

## The Yellow King RPG (2020)

Robin D. Laws designed a dedicated RPG based on the King in Yellow, with four different settings spanning different time periods:
- **Paris 1895** — The original Chambers setting
- **Dalem City 1995** — A modern urban setting
- **Paris 2015** — A contemporary art-world setting
- **Carcosa** — A dimension-hopping setting

## Video Games

- **Call of Cthulhu: Dark Corners of the Earth** — References the Yellow Sign
- **The Sinking City** — Features Carcosa and the King in Yellow
- **Bloodborne** — While not directly adapting Chambers, the game's cosmic horror and "Great Ones" share DNA with the King in Yellow mythos
